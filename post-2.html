Hiring Portal - FITE

“FiTE” is an AI-equipped matching portal independently designed and developed by Profound Vision. Automatic translation of application information for overseas ICT personnel and job information for Japanese companies. Furthermore, we use AI to index the degree of correlation and present candidates with high scores, increasing the accuracy and speed of matching.

The system analyzes linguistic and nonverbal information such as "voice," "words," "content," and "language structure" that candidates give during interviews. By automatically calculating the evaluation results by interview professionals, we are able to reduce the selection process.

The solution provided specifically for Japanese companies looking for young ICT engineers across Pakistan using a extensive vetting process, is long road to Pakistan-Japan friendly diplomatic relations. Currently, FITE is evolving into AI-equipped matching portal for Japanese companies. The candidate registrations are mostly done, along with document verification process through the system. Furthermore, jobs are being placed into the portal as well , which further sums up project’s huge potential.

In this system, as a LAMP stack engineer, I took part with major stakeholders for setting up the workflows of hiring process and also provided the structure to make it work. Also, I helped implement the wireframed design for showing the workflows inaction.
